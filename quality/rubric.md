# Documentation Quality Rubric

Based on Treude et al. (2020) ten-dimension rubric.

## Structure

| Dimension | 0 — Poor | 1 — Adequate | 2 — Good |
|-----------|----------|---------------|----------|
| **Findability** | Cannot be located via navigation or search | Findable with effort | Clearly linked and searchable |
| **Completeness** | Critical gaps present | Covers main points | All expected content present |
| **Consistency** | Format varies wildly | Mostly consistent | Uniform structure and terminology |
| **Currency** | Outdated for >1 release | Updated within current release | Up-to-date and versioned |

## Content

| Dimension | 0 — Poor | 1 — Adequate | 2 — Good |
|-----------|----------|---------------|----------|
| **Accuracy** | Contains errors | Mostly correct | Verified and tested |
| **Clarity** | Confusing or ambiguous | Understandable with effort | Clear to target audience |
| **Relevance** | Irrelevant content | Mostly on-topic | Focused on reader goal |

## Style

| Dimension | 0 — Poor | 1 — Adequate | 2 — Good |
|-----------|----------|---------------|----------|
| **Readability** | Dense walls of text | Some structure | Scannable, headings, lists |
| **Grammar** | Frequent errors | Minor issues | Professional quality |
| **Accessibility** | No alt text, poor contrast | Basic accessibility | WCAG 2.1 AA compliant |

## Scoring

- **Pass:** Score ≥ 14/20 and no critical gaps
- **Needs revision:** Score 10–13
- **Reject:** Score < 10
